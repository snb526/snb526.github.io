Easy Homemade Pie Crust


Description

Butter makes this buttery flaky recipe the perfect crust for your pie!

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Total Time: 4 Hours 15 minutes
Yield: (1) 9 inch pie crust

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 173 calories; 11.7 g fat; 14.9 g carbohydrates; 2.1 g protein; 31 mg cholesterol; 155 mg sodium.

Ingredients

1 1/4 Cups All-purpose flour
1/4 Teaspoon Salt
1/2 Cup Butter, Chilled (one stick)
1/4 Cup Ice Water

Equipment

Cookware Large Bowl
2 Knives
Spoon
Plastic Wrap
Measurings 1 Cup
1/2 Cup
1/4 Cup
1/4 Teaspoon

Steps:


Combine the 1 1/4 cup of flour with the 1/4 teaspoon of salt into a large bowl.


diceto cut food into even, small squares about 1/4" in diameter
the 1/2 cup of butter (one stick) into small cubes.


Add the cubes of butter to the flour mixture. Using the 2 knives, cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.


Stir in the 1/4 cup of water a tablespoon at a time until mixture forms into a ball.


Wrap the ball of dough in plastic and refrigerate for 4 hours, or overnight.


When ready to use, roll the dough out with a rolling pin to fit a 9 inch pie plate. Place crust in pie plate. Press the dough evenly into the bottom and sides of the pie plate.
