Pumpkin Pie


Description

This is the ideal formula for a rich pumpkin pie that's also much less likely to crack on top. Serve garnished with whipped cream and freshly grated nutmeg.

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 45 minutes
Total Time: 1 hour
Yield: (1) 9 inch pie
Servings: 8

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 320 calories; 14.2 g fat; 41.9 g carbohydrates; 7.6 g protein; 117 mg cholesterol; 465 mg sodium.

Ingredients

1 15 Ounce Can Pumpkin Puree
3 Egg Yolks
1 Large Egg
1 (14 ounce) can Sweetened Condensed Milk
1 Teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 Teaspoon ginger
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1/4 Teaspoon Nutmeg
1/8 Teaspoon Chinese 5-spice
1 9-inch Unbaked Pie Crust (or store bought)

Equipment

Cookware Medium Bowl
Saucepan
Mesh Strainer
Hand Mixer
Knife or Skewer
Springform Pan* (optional)
or Regular Cake Pan
Heating Element Oven
Measurings 1 Cup
1/2 Cup
1 Teaspoon
1 Tablespoon

Steps:


Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).


Separate 3 eggs and put their yolks in your large mixing bowl.


Add and whisk together the can of pumpkin puree and egg until it becomes smooth.


Add the can of sweetened condensed milk, 1 teaspoon of cinnamon, 1/2 teaspoon of ginger, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg, and 1/8 teaspoon of Chinese 5-spice powder.
Whisk the ingredients until thoroughly combined.


Fit your homemade pie crust into a 9-inch pie plate.
A store bought frozen pie crust works just as well.


Pour the filling into the pie crust.
Lightly tap it on the work surface to release any air bubbles.


Bake the pie in the preheated 425 degree oven for 15 minutes.
